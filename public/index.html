Our Story

Where wine, gathering,
and memory meet

We believe gatherings, paired with wine, provide the greatest opportunity to create connections and memories. Set among the vines of Valle de Guadalupe, Aroma Events was built for guests to live in the moment, indulge in one another, and celebrate the aroma of life.

Every detail — the light, the pour, the table — is arranged so the day is remembered less for what was planned and more for how it felt.

The Space

Three settings, one valley

Indoor and open-air spaces designed to move with the day — for an intimate dinner of twenty or a celebration of two hundred.

The Vineyard Terrace

Open-air, framed by rows of vines and golden-hour light — built for ceremonies and long, unhurried dinners.

The Barrel Room

An intimate, candlelit indoor space among the barrels — ideal for tastings, rehearsal dinners, and smaller gatherings.

The Garden Courtyard
