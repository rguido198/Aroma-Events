Milestone Celebrations

Milestone Celebrations

Birthdays, anniversaries, and reunions — an occasion made a little more memorable.

Some celebrations don't need a full production — they need the right setting. A milestone birthday, an anniversary, a family reunion that only happens once every few years: Aroma Events works for the celebrations that deserve more than a restaurant reservation but don't need a wedding-scale production. The valley does a lot of the work on its own — golden light, open sky, a table set among the vines.

What It's Good For

Milestone celebrations here run smaller and looser than weddings by default — there's no ceremony to choreograph, so the day can be built entirely around the meal, the toasts, and the people in the room. Families use it for reunions that bring people in from out of town; friends use it for a fortieth or fiftieth birthday that wants a real setting instead of a rented banquet hall; couples use it for a vow renewal or anniversary dinner.

The format flexes to whatever the occasion calls for — a seated dinner, a cocktail-style gathering, or something in between.

Recommended Spaces

The Garden Courtyard suits most milestone celebrations — flexible enough for a seated dinner or a standing cocktail-style gathering. Larger reunions or milestone parties can use the Vineyard Terrace; smaller, more intimate celebrations often choose the Barrel Room.

  • Guest range: 10–200
  • Works for daytime or evening celebrations

Frequently Asked Questions

It ranges widely — from an intimate dinner of a dozen in the Barrel Room to a reunion of 200 on the Vineyard Terrace.

Yes, many hosts do — see our Wine Dinners & Tastings page for that format.

Do you host vow renewals or anniversary dinners?

Yes — these are some of the most common milestone celebrations we host.
